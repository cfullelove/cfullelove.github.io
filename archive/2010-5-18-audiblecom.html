I’ve mentioned several times books that I have read (listened to) from Audible.com. Since signing up for their Platinum account, I’ve gone through 12 books in under 2.5 months. I am a very big fan of this service, and I have decided to become an Audible.com affiliate.

Being an affiliate means that I will be able to put Audible.com links on this site, and get a small amount of credit for any sales that result. I am NOT going to be cramming ads into every viewable portion of the site - I still want people to come and read my blog.

My plan is this:

I have added a link to Audible.com in the right-hand sidebar, which you should be able to see. This links to Audible.com’s Gold Membership signup page. For a monthly fee, you receive credits for 1 book per month, plus get discounted prices on any book you purchase on top of that. It’s a very good deal.

In addition to the sidebar link, every book I write about from now on, I’ll put a link down the bottom where you can purchase it.

Here is a link to the last book I wrote about, as an example:

 

Super Crunchers
