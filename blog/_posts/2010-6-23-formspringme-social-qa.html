On recent episode of net@night (hosted by Leo Laporte & Amber MacArthur), an interesting new website Formspring.me was discussed. Formspring is a really cool service. Basically, you have a profile where people can ask you questions, and you can answer them. Simple as that.

It may sound a bit dull or pointless, but through reading other’s answers to questions on their profile, you can really learn alot about a person. Not only that, if you are someone that never knows what to talk about, you can create content purely from reacting to questions people ask.

You can create an account on the Formspring site, or log in using Facebook Connect. From there, search for people you know already using the service, invite others to join, and start asking and answering questions.
