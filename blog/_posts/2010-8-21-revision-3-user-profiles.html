The Internet TV site Revision3 has recently introduced user profiles. I’ve only just signed up, so I don’t know all of the features yet, but there is some really cool stuff included.

The structure seems to be very “social mediaish” - ie it gives you a public profile (here’s mine) and allows you to follow other users. All very cool I think. Its kinda like a niche Youtube.

Another really cool feature that I am excited about is the ability to create a custom feed of Rev3 shows I like. You can see an my setup below:

this link to get a feed of my favourites. I use this link to subscribe to my favourite shows in iTunes. But I would also encourage you to go and create an account yourself and have a look around to see if any of the shows interest you.

I am a big fan of Revision3 and their shows. Most of the shows are released on a weekly basis, but there are some that have smaller more frequent releases. I really like their style of production and the fact that everything is freely available for download or streaming. Even their sponsors are decent! (though perhaps a little repetitive)

These are the shows I watch on a semi-regular basis.
