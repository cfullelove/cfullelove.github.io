I have wanted to start a blog for quite along time. However, I never really thought I would have enough to talk about, or the skills to do so. In actual fact those that know me would probably say I still don't have the skills... But I do now have things to talk about, so I'm going to give it a try.

Firstly, I should probably give a little bit of background on myself. I am a 22 year old student studying Electrical Engineering at the University of Queensland in Australia. I enjoy, for the most part, what I study, though I am rather easily distracted. One such distraction led to my interest in the hospitality industry. I have worked in a Pizza Capers store for the last three years, and for most of the last two years I have served as a manager and Tech Support. I also work for the Queensland Department of Main Roads in Nerang, QLD. I live in Brisbane, and travel the 1 hour each way three days a week.
My recent motivation for blogging came from reading, or rather listening to, "What Would Google Do?", from Jeff Jarvis (BuzzMachine Blog). The travelling I do make listening to audiobooks an ideal way for me to read. I heard about this book while listening to TWiG, a program on the TWiT network run by Leo Laporte. I found the book incredibly interesting and very thought provoking. I had to rewind the audiobook more than a few times because my mind would go off thinking about what what just said. I liked how Jarvis spoke about the internet being a conversation, and basically I decided I wanted to join.

One rule I intend to follow when blogging is to keep my posts short. I must confess that when I read blogs, the one one of the most important factors in whether I read it or not is its length. On that note, this ends my first blog. PLEASE feel free to leave comments and suggestions.
