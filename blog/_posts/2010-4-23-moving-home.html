You may have noticed a few changes to the layout of the blog over the last 24 hours. I’ve moved hosting to Squarespace. This service provides a much more feature rich experience for me, as well as hopefully better reading experience for readers.


I’m using the Pro package on Squarespace. This means I can assign my own domainname as well as a few extra features probably not evident to the public. It isn’t a free service… it costs ~$12 a month. I do believe this is a justifiable expense though. I rationalise this by thinking of other purchases I have made and the cost forms a small percentage of my overall expenses. I also am hoping that one day I will be able to actually generate money from this site…

The RSS feed for this blog has already been transferred. You may notice read items become unread again though. The feed items will point to the new site.

The www.fullelove.com and blog.fullelove.com domainnames are currently in the process of transferring to Squarespace. This means tha the blogger site will no longer be active, and should redirect to the new site.

Feel free to share your opinions in the comments regarding the move.
