I am now the owner of an iPad. Well, technically anyway. My parents are currently in the US on business, and it just so happens they found themselves in an Apple Store, and they pickup one up for me!

I was planning on waiting for the release of the iPad in Australia, but apparently the release date has been pushed back by a month. Being more than a little curious about the iPad, I was always going to jump at the chance at getting one a little early.

I am very much looking forward to getting my hands on it. I have read (and listened to) literally 10s, perhaps 100s of reviews and commentary on the subject. I have yet to hear anything substantial enough to put me off wanting one.


The only drawback for getting the iPad early in Australia, is that the AppStore is not yet fully up to date. This means that I may not be able to get some of the Apps that I have been hearing about. There could be a solution though! I have heard if you purchase a US iTunes Gift Voucher it will enable you to make purchases from the US, thus making the ocean of new apps available.

@LisaTickledPink, the winner of one of the TWiT iPads, lives in New Zealand. I've been following her tweets and it seems there is way to get to the apps... we shall see!

My family arrives back in Brisbane on Sunday, so I don't have long to wait. I will definitely be keeping this blog up to date with my experiences, so stay tuned!
