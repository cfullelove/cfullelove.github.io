I had the pleasure of going for a decent half-day road trip to Queen Mary Falls this weekend. It’s a truly beautiful part of Southern Queensland - just about the NSW border.

Sadly, the trip was on somewhat of a whim, so I didn’t have a decent camera. But my HTC Desire did manage to take some passable shots.

This photo shows off the countryside and the weather on the day.

All in all, it was a fantastic drive. I saw hundreds of cows, some horses and a few wallabies (and managed not to hit any either). If you are looking to get away from the stress of the next few weeks, I recommend going somewhere like this.
